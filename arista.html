What is ARISTA?

ARISTA is Stuyvesant High School's chapter of the National Honor Society. It is an organization that strives to uphold its four pillars of character, leadership, scholarship, and service. Its members constantly give back to the community and dedicate countless hours to be role models and leaders of the future.

How to be in ARISTA

In order to be a join, you must submit an application detailing their extracurriculars and write a specified essay. You can only apply during the February/March of the sophmore and junior years. In order to even be considered, you must also have an overall GPA of 93. There are no exceptions. The application form can be found on the sidebar as soon as it becomes available.
